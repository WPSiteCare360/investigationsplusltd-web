<p><strong>Toronto, ON –&nbsp;</strong><a href="https://investigationhotline.org/">Investigation Hotline</a>&nbsp;is pleased to announce it has becoming one of the most sought-after private investigation teams in Ontario.</p>
<p>Investigation Hotline is a private investigation and detective agency headquartered in Toronto, Canada.&nbsp; The company’s diverse investigation team is made up of professional investigators, forensic examiners, detectives, and security experts.&nbsp; Since 1988, Investigation Hotline has been using the latest and greatest intelligence-gathering procedures and techniques to seek out and gather detailed court-worthy case reports for its clients.</p>
<p>In the company’s most recent news, Investigation Hotline is announcing that, as a result of its impressive five-star rating, it has become one of Ontario’s most trending private investigation teams.</p>
<p>“According to our clients, the number of repeat customers we have and their positive reviews, it has been a humbling experience to know we’ve become such a trusted resource,” says founder of Investigation Hotline, Mitchell Dubros.&nbsp; “We pride ourselves in our utmost level of trust, legality, and professionalism for personal, corporate, or domestic cases – always working with absolute discretion.”</p>
<p>Investigation Hotline provides clients with a host of professional services, including:</p>
<ul>
<li>Consulting and intelligence services</li>
<li>Fraud and forgery case investigations</li>
<li>Security and protection cases</li>
<li>Missing persons</li>
<li>Anonymous threats</li>
<li>Surveillance and computer detailing</li>
<li>International private investigations</li>
<li>Litigation cases – employee theft, fraud, money laundering and stolen property</li>
<li>Cheating spouses and child custody cases</li>
<li>And more!</li>
</ul>
