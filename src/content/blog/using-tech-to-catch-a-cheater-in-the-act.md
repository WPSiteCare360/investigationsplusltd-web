<p>While there has always been debate on how many people in monogamous relationships end up cheating on their partner, estimates range between 15 and 50%. Being the victim of a cheating partner can be mentally and emotionally devastating and can have long-lasting effects that can follow you into new relationships and situations. Regardless of how negatively this could impact someone’s life, studies estimate that many people still continue to cheat on their partners and will likely continue to do so.</p>
