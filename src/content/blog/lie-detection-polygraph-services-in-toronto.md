<h2 class="text-text-100 mt-3 -mb-1 text-[1.125rem] font-bold">The Limitations of Polygraph Equipment</h2>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">Polygraph machines measure physiological responses: heart rate, blood pressure, breathing patterns. They record stress, not dishonesty.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">The problem? Polygraphs can be manipulated. Countermeasures work. The machine tracks physical reactions but misses the subtle indicators that reveal actual deception.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">Our investigators catch what machines cannot:</p>
<ul class="[li_&amp;]:mb-0 [li_&amp;]:mt-1 [li_&amp;]:gap-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ul]:pb-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ol]:pb-1 list-disc flex flex-col gap-1 pl-8 mb-3">
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>Automatic verbal responses</strong> that slip out before conscious filtering</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>Linguistic patterns</strong> including pronoun shifts, tense changes, and excess detail</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>Speech rhythm disruptions</strong> when moving between truth and fabrication</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>Cognitive load indicators</strong> shown through hesitation and processing delays</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>Deflection strategies</strong> used to avoid direct answers</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>Emotional-verbal mismatches</strong> between stated feelings and actual presentation</li>
</ul>
