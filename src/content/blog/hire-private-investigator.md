<h4><strong>Employment Background Screening: Check before Hire</strong></h4>
<p>Private investigators play a crucial role in vetting potential employees, whether they are local or international candidates. Detailed scrutiny is necessary to ensure the competence and qualifications of prospective hires.</p>
<p>PIs can verify the accuracy of the specialist&#8217;s employment history by contacting previous employers or reviewing employment records. This helps confirm the duration of employment, job responsibilities, and reasons for leaving previous positions.</p>

This ensures that the qualifications claimed by the individual are legitimate and up-to-date.</p>
<p>By employing a combination of these investigative techniques, private investigator can help verify the qualifications and reputation of a specialist, mitigate the risk of CV falsification, and provide valuable insights to clients making hiring decisions.</p>
<h3><strong>Prevent Potential Problems: Fraud Investigation Unveils 2024 Crime Trends </strong></h3>
<p>Below are four crime trends emerging in 2024, discovered through our extensive professional experiences:</p>
<h4><strong>Cybercrime</strong></h4>
<p>Cybercrime has surged notably in recent years with the widespread use of technology and the internet. This encompasses a range of illegal activities like hacking, phishing, identity theft, and ransomware attacks. Credit card fraud is a common cybercrime where stolen card details are used for unauthorized purchases.</p>
<h4><strong>Fraud</strong></h4>
<p>Fraud, a broad term encompassing intentional deception leading to financial or other losses, is another significant trend. Types include investment fraud, insurance fraud, and identity theft. Private Investigators employ diverse techniques such as surveillance, background checks, and forensic accounting to uncover fraudulent activities, particularly identity theft, which involves the unauthorized use of personal information for fraudulent purposes. Regular monitoring of credit reports and cautiousness with personal information are advised preventive measures.</p>
<h4><strong>Identity Theft</strong></h4>
<p>Identity theft, involving the unauthorized use of personal information, has seen a rise in recent years. Cybercriminals exploit stolen identities for various fraudulent activities, posing serious threats to victims&#8217; financial well-being. Private Investigators play a crucial role in identifying and tracking down identity thieves, employing various investigative tools and techniques. Preventive measures include safeguarding personal information and vigilant monitoring of credit reports and bank statements.</p>
<h4><strong>Workplace Theft</strong></h4>
<p>Workplace theft, referring to the theft of company assets by employees or insiders, remains a noticeable trend. This includes theft of inventory, equipment, and intellectual property, significantly impacting a company&#8217;s financial health. Private Investigations Plus investigates such cases, uncovering theft instances and aiding companies in asset recovery efforts.</p>
